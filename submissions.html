SUBMISSIONS

Submit to ASTRO

STATUS: OPEN for photo essays; OPEN for written content.

ASTRO publishes written content, photo essays and video. We are interested in pieces that capture the sense of finding oneself in a new place and all the feelings that accompany that. In your submissions we hope to find something familiar, something to connect to, a shared experience, a strong sense of place, character and time.

Our submissions come from all types of creatives and others; journalists, anthropologists, photographers, poets, painters etc. But most of all we are interested in stories that can bring together anyone who has left their home and has created a new one somewhere else and can help us build a community.

We are a small team and 100% of our content comes from submissions. We see ourselves as a nurturing, welcoming digital gallery space in which anyone, with or without a portfolio, can share their experiences, engage and entertain readers. We want to make you part of a community of modern migrants, a community with which we all share something in common, but also to make your content look amazing and show it to the world!

Written submissions and Photo essays for ASTRO are accepted at any time from September to June. We will read your work and respond to you within 1 month.
